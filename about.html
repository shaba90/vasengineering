Quality Assurance

Our quality assurance activities include a planned system of review procedures conducted by personnel not directly involved in the inventory compilation/development process. These reviews verify that data quality objectives were met, ensure that the inventory represents the best materials given the current state of scientific knowledge and data available, and support the effectiveness of the project.

Health & Safety

At VAS ENGINEERING CONSULTS LIMITED every area of our operation has a safety policy: site organization, work at height, structural stability, cranes, electricity, fire, mobile plant and vehicles and demolition. Our health and safety policy objective is to provide and maintain a safe and healthy workplace, safe guard plants and equipment and implement practical safe systems of work. VAS ENGINEERING CONSULTS LIMITED is committed to providing the safest possible working environment to anybody entering our sites, which include employees, contractors, customers or visitors. We maintain a culture where workplace accidents are preventable — for which all our people are responsible.
